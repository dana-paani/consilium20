Consilium’20 is the 6th edition of the Annual Business Conclave of NSUT, organised by the Finance and Economics Society (FES). With a line of eminent speakers and thought provoking events, Consilium has gained the stature of being a platform for young minds to come together and deliberate upon the ever changing corporate world. Consilium has always been one of the biggest and most competitive Business Conclaves in the Delhi Circuit providing business enthusiasts a platform to connect,interact and engage.

The Finance and Economics society of NSUT is a dynamic platform where people discuss and deliberate over business ethics, stock market and burning issues in the finance and economics world. At FES, we believe that it's important that every individual, irrespective of field not only gets acquainted with basics of finance and economics but we aim to nurture an interest for the same.

Do you desire to be a top consultant? Have you ever been curious about business decision-making?
If yes, then this is just the right place for you to be.

We welcome you to come and be a part of Delhi’s most enthralling case study event; where you get to tackle some of the biggest industries with your analytical and problem-solving skills. Madurik : The Policy Case Event

EconomicsCPI

Ever wondered what goes into making a policy? How it feels to be a policymaker? No need to look any further.

The Finance & Economics Society Of NSUT is proud to present Maudrik - the Economics event of Consilium- The NSUT Business Conclave. With a myriad of problems gripping the present day economy, the country is in dire need of young minds to redefine and devise new economic policies.

A one of its kind policy making event, Maudrik gives you the opportunity to step into the shoes of a policymaker and put your analytical and creative abilities to test.

The competition consists of two rounds:

Preliminary Round : This is an online round wherein a case study would be given to the registered participants and they have to identify the problem, analyse and suggest effective policy solutions .
The case will be released on 6th January 2020
Deadline for submission: 19th January 2020

On Campus round : The shortlisted teams would be given a new case which they'll present in the on-campus round on 2nd Feb, 2020. Further details would be mailed to the finalists.

Rules & Regulations:
The event is open to all undergraduate students.
Participation is allowed in teams comprising of a maximum of 3 members.
Lone Teams and cross-college teams are also permitted.
